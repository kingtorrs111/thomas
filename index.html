Event Overview

SMCC-CCIS Week 2025 is a yearly campus event that showcases student initiatives, industry discussions and practical workshops on cybersecurity, software development, artificial intelligence, among other things.

Join us for a week of learning, networking, and fun as we celebrate innovation!

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Is the event open to all students?
Yes, the event is open to all departments and year levels.

2. Do I need to register?
Registration is required for competitions and workshops.

3. Is there a registration fee?
No, all SMCC-CCIS Week activities are free of charge.
